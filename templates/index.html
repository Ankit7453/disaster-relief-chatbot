Disaster Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Drop, cover, and hold on
  • Stay away from windows
  • If outdoors, move to open areas
  • If in vehicle, pull over safely

Don't

  • Don't run outside during shaking
  • Don't use elevators
  • Don't light matches or candles
  • Don't stand near furniture that can fall

Do

  • Move to higher ground
  • Listen to emergency broadcasts
  • Turn off utilities if instructed
  • Prepare an emergency kit

Don't

  • Don't walk through flowing water
  • Don't drive through flooded areas
  • Don't touch electrical equipment if wet
  • Don't return home until authorities say it's safe

Do

  • Listen for unusual sounds
  • Evacuate if safe to do so
  • Watch for flooding after landslide
  • Report broken utilities to authorities

Don't

  • Don't stay in the path of a landslide
  • Don't return to affected area until cleared
  • Don't approach downed power lines
  • Don't build near steep slopes
